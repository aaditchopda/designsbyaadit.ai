How I Work

From brief to
brand.

A clear, collaborative process so you always know what's happening — and what's coming next.

01
Discovery
Understanding your brand
We start with a detailed brief — your business, your audience, your competitors, and what makes you different. The more I understand your world, the better the design will represent it.
Brand Brief Competitor Research Mood Board
Approx. 1–2 days
02
Concept
Sketching the direction
I explore multiple creative directions before committing to one. You receive initial concepts to review — we pick the strongest direction together and refine from there.
Initial Concepts Direction Review Feedback Round
Approx. 2–3 days
03
Design
Crafting the final identity
The chosen direction is refined into a complete, polished design. Every detail — spacing, weight, colour, proportion — is considered intentionally. Up to 3–4 revision rounds are included to ensure the design meets your vision and expectations.
Logo Refinement Color System Typography Revisions
Approx. 3–5 days
04
Delivery
Handing over your brand
All final files packaged neatly and delivered. Every format you will ever need — print, digital, social. Plus clear instructions on how to use your new identity correctly.
All File Formats Brand Guidelines Usage Instructions
Approx. 1 day

Please note: All timelines indicated above are approximate and serve as a general reference. Actual delivery may vary depending on the complexity of the project, the number of revision rounds, and the timeliness of client feedback. I am committed to delivering high-quality work and will communicate proactively should any adjustments to the timeline be required.
